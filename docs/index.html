Why DORA Dashboard

Built for teams that can't use SaaS analytics

Everything runs inside your estate — auditable, air-gap-friendly, and branded to your organization.

Self-hosted & auditable

No data leaves your network. Documented metric formulas and access audit logging for compliance.

Enterprise SSO

Azure Entra ID (OIDC) single sign-on plus GitHub OAuth, with role-based access control.

GitHub + Jira, correlated

End-to-end lead and cycle time by joining GitHub delivery events to Jira work items.

Docker & Helm

Hardened container image with docker-compose for small deploys and a Helm chart for Kubernetes.

Configurable & auditable

Admins define what counts as a deployment or failure, the rolling window, and Elite/High/Medium bands — every change audited, with lineage on each metric.

Exportable reports

One-click CSV and branded PDF delivery reports — every metric with its tier, drill-down breakdowns, and an auto-generated “needs attention” section.

Per-team filtering

Map squads to their GitLab projects and Jira keys, then slice the whole dashboard, breakdowns and report by team — with per-team targets and benchmark bands.

Scheduled digest

Push a recurring delivery digest to email (PDF attached) or a Teams/Slack webhook on a cron — summary, tiers and “needs attention”, all in-cluster.
